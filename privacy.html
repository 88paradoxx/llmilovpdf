Privacy Policy

We believe privacy is a fundamental human right. Our architecture is designed to enforce it.

Local Processing Guarantee

When you use iLovPDF, your files (images and PDFs) are processed locally within your web browser. We do not upload your documents to any cloud server for processing. This means your sensitive data never leaves your device's memory.

Minimal Analytics

We collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., "PDF tool opened") to help us identify bugs and improve performance. This telemetry never includes file names, document content, or personally identifiable information.

Common Questions

Do you store my files?

No. Since we don't have a backend database for file storage, it is technically impossible for us to store your files. They vanish from memory the moment you close the tab.

Do you use cookies?

We use minimal local storage to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode). That's it.
