About iLovPDF

Local-First Architecture

iLovPDF is built on a revolutionary local-first architecture. Unlike traditional online PDF tools that upload your sensitive documents to remote servers for processing, iLovPDF handles everything directly within your web browser.

This means your files never leave your device. Whether you're editing a legal contract, compressing a personal photo, or converting a financial report, the data processing happens entirely on your machine using advanced WebAssembly technologies.

Built for Professionals

We designed iLovPDF for designers, developers, marketers, and teams who demand speed and security. Our suite of tools—including the structural PDF editor and AI-powered background remover—offers desktop-class performance without the need to install bloatware.

Key Principles

100% Client-Side

Zero server uploads for document processing. Your privacy is mathematically guaranteed by the architecture.

No Sign-Ups

Frictionless access. No accounts, no paywalls, no email harvesting. Just open and edit.

Modern UX

A stunning, dark-mode interface powered by the latest web standards for fluid interaction.

FAQ

Is iLovPDF an official desktop application? +
No. iLovPDF runs entirely in your modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari) and relies on your device's processing power.
Do you store my documents? +
Absolutely not. Since there are no server-side processing components for file manipulation, we physically cannot store or view your files. They reside in your browser's memory only while you edit them.
